Genii Solar Solutions

GENII’s development process involves systematic utilization of state-of-the-art knowledge and expertise gained from research toward the production of useful materials, devices, systems, recycle the waste, creation of energy from sustainable source or methods and includes design & development of prototypes to help mass-scale production.

GENII is a major promoter of Renewable Energy in Urban, Rural & Remote area applications of Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) & Rural Electrification Corporation (REC) under Ministry Power, Govt. of India, and is associated with various state and central government projects across the country in remote areas of various states such as Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, Assam, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Arunachal Pradesh.

GENII is associated with central and state government organizations to promote renewable energy through organizing conferences, presentations and local level awareness complaining.
