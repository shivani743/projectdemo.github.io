Solar Energy

Genii, as major promoter of Renewable Energy in Urban, Rural & Remote area applications of Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) & Rural Electrification Corporation (REC) under Ministry Power, Govt of India, and associated with various state and central government project across the country including maitray and paramilitary forces.


Genii has commissioned MWs of ON Grid, OFF Grid, Micro & Mini-Grid Solar PV Power Plants in various states and of various capacities across the country. GES is proud to be associated in lighting thousands of remote houses in many villages under DDUGJY scheme of GoI. Majority of work includes construction of control room, setting up of Solar Power Plant, Laying LT Line, Power distribution, electrical wiring in each house. Remote Village Electrification is done under Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY) by REC, GoI in association with OREDA, Odisha.

Integrated Solar Roofing System.

GES is associated with Global leaders in the field of Solar Energy to provide best quality systems to our customers, least downtime and ensure quality service. We are actively participating in the Jawaharlal Nehru National Solar Mission under Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), GOI, New Delhi for promoting Green Energy by executing prestigious projects in both Government and Private sector. The initiative by Govt of India for capacity creation of 100GW Solar Power by 2020 has provided a new thrust to the Solar Program.

Integrated Solar Roofing System.

We are associated with world leaders like SMA Germany, Refusol, Fronius India, Consul Neowatt, Statcon Energiaa to provide world class technology for Solar Power Control Units (PCU) and advanced power electronics. For solar PV modules, we are associated with Waaree Group Consortium, M/s. Vikram Solar Ltd and other leading manufacturer.
